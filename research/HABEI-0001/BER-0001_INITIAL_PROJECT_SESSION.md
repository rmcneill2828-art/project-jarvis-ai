# 3. Key Findings

## 3.1 Engineering Behaviour Preceded Governance

The initial session showed engineering behaviour before formal governance was fully established.

The collaboration began to distinguish intent, scope, repository structure and implementation discipline before AIEMS was mature.

## 3.2 AIEMS Principles Emerged From Observed Practice

AIEMS principles appear to have emerged from repeated observed practice rather than from purely abstract process definition.

The session provided early evidence that practical engineering behaviour could be converted into governance principles.

## 3.3 Repository-First Thinking and Engineering Restraint Emerged Early

Repository-first thinking and engineering restraint appeared early in the project.

The collaboration showed early movement toward preserving repository integrity, avoiding uncontrolled change and separating future ideas from immediate implementation.

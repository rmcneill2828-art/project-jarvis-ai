# Gate Outcome Classification

Every phase gate shall conclude with one of the following outcomes:

| Outcome | Definition | Progression |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| **PASS** | All entry and exit criteria have been successfully met. No outstanding issues prevent progression. | Proceed to the next phase. |
| **PASS WITH CONDITIONS** | Phase objectives have been substantially achieved, however agreed actions must be completed or monitored during the next phase. | Proceed subject to completion of recorded actions. |
| **HOLD** | One or more mandatory exit criteria have not been satisfied. Further work is required before the phase can be reassessed. | Do not proceed until outstanding items are completed and the gate is reviewed again. |
| **FAIL** | The phase has not achieved its objectives or significant issues prevent progression. The phase must be re-planned and repeated before further progression. | Progression is not authorised. |

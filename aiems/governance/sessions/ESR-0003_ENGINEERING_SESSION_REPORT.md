# 10. Engineering Lessons Learned

ESR-0003 produced the following engineering lessons:

1. Repository verification should be independent of implementation.
2. Repository execution responsibilities are best assigned to the Engineering Implementer when explicitly authorised.
3. Engineering quality improves through iterative review and convergence rather than single-pass implementation.
4. Engineering work should be organised around cohesive engineering objectives where this reduces unnecessary workstreams without increasing engineering risk.
5. AIEMS governing principles remained stable while operational practices evolved through evidence-led improvement.
